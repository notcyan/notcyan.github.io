Game Developer & Generalist

Hello! I'm Corey, also known as NotCyan online, I'm a Brisbane based hobbyist game developer with over 10 years of experience. With a background spanning various game engines and programming languages, I focus on creating engaging player experiences through innovative gameplay mechanics and thoughtful design.

I specialise in:

  • Programming - Extensive knowledge in Unity C#, C++ and Unreal Engine Blueprints
  • Editor Tools - Building useful Unity editor extensions for faster development
  • Game Design - Designing compelling game mechanics and player progression systems
  • 3D Modeling - Experience in Blender for designing game-ready assets
  • Project Managment - Creating dynamic enemy behaviors and ecosystem simulations

My approach combines technical expertise with creative vision, allowing me to tackle complex challenges while maintaining a focus on player enjoyment. Whether working solo or contributing to a team, I bring dedication, adaptability, and a commitment to quality to every project.

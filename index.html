About Me

Game Designer & Programmer with a Passion for Innovation

Hello! I'm Corey Horne, a passionate game developer with expertise in both design and programming. With a background spanning various game engines and programming languages, I focus on creating engaging player experiences through innovative gameplay mechanics and thoughtful design.

I specialize in:

  • Game Design - Crafting compelling game mechanics and player progression systems
  • Programming - Implementing gameplay systems in C# and Blueprints
  • AI Development - Creating dynamic enemy behaviors and ecosystem simulations
  • Level Design - Building engaging environments that guide player experience
  • 3D Modeling - Designing game-ready assets to bring worlds to life

My approach combines technical expertise with creative vision, allowing me to tackle complex challenges while maintaining a focus on player enjoyment. Whether working solo or leading a team, I bring dedication, adaptability, and a commitment to quality to every project.

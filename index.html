About Me

Game Developer & Unity Specialist

Hello! I'm Corey, also known as NotCyan online, I'm a Brisbane based game developer with over 10 years of experience making games. I specialise in gameplay programming, UI programming, engine tool development, and systems architecture. In addition, through many solo projects, I've gained experience in many aspects of game development including design, art & 3D modelling, and audio engineering from an intermediate to professional standard.

I specialise in:

  • Programming - Extensive knowledge in Unity C#, Unreal Engine Blueprints and C++
  • Game Design - Designing compelling game mechanics and player progression systems
  • 3D Modeling - Experience in Blender for creating game-ready assets
  • Editor Tools - Building useful Unity editor extensions for faster development
  • Project Managment - Project roadmaps & sprint managment

My approach combines technical expertise with creative vision, allowing me to tackle complex challenges while maintaining a focus on player enjoyment. Whether working solo or contributing to a team, I bring dedication, adaptability, and a commitment to quality to every project.
